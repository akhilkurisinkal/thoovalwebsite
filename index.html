How it works

From blank page to live blog in three steps.

01

Write in the editor

Thooval's WYSIWYG editor shows your writing exactly as it'll appear — bold is bold, headings look like headings. Under the hood, everything saves as plain .md files on your Mac. Your words, your files, fully offline.

02

Connect your GitHub

Link your free GitHub account once. Thooval uses it to host your blog — no servers to rent, no hosting bills. GitHub Pages serves your site to the world for free.

03

One-click publish

Hit Publish. Thooval builds a clean, fast static blog from your writing and pushes it live instantly. Your readers get a beautiful site — you get zero maintenance.

Why developers switch

What frustrates Ghost users most

💸

The renewal bill

Ghost's Starter plan feels reasonable at $9/month. Then you hit 1,000 members. Then the plan you need is $25/month. Ghost's pricing scales with your success.

🔒

Your content isn't portable

Ghost's export format is JSON. Your posts aren't stored as Markdown on your machine — they live in Ghost's database. Migration is painful by design.

🌐

You're paying for hosting you don't need

GitHub Pages hosts static sites for free. If your blog is text and images, you have zero reason to pay $108+/year for a managed server.

📝

The editor isn't offline-first

Ghost's editor requires an internet connection. Thooval drafts are local .md files — write on a plane, publish when you land.

🧩

Integrations cost extra

Ghost charges for newsletters, analytics, and comments. Thooval has Giscus and Google Analytics built in, at no extra cost, ever.

📡

You don't control your infrastructure

Ghost decides when to update, when to migrate, and when to shut down. Your blog lives on their servers. With Thooval, your blog lives on GitHub.
